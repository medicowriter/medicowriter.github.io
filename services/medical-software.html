Custom Tech for Healthcare

Effective medical writing often requires effective data management. Our in-house software engineering team builds custom tools to streamline your data collection, storage, and reporting processes. We understand that off-the-shelf software often doesn't fit the unique workflows of hospitals or specialized research groups.

We build secure, compliant, and intuitive applications that talk the language of doctors and researchers.

Our Technical Capabilities

  • Patient Registries: Custom databases to track patient outcomes and real-world evidence (RWE).
  • Hospital Data Management: Streamlining electronic health records (EHR) and departmental reports.
  • Automated Reporting Tools: Generating standard reports from raw data with one click.
  • Data Visualization Dashboards: Interactive dashboards for monitoring trial progress or hospital KPIs.
  • Custom EDC Systems: Lightweight Electronic Data Capture systems for smaller studies.

Security & Compliance

We prioritize data security and integrity. Our solutions are designed with HIPAA and GDPR considerations in mind, ensuring that sensitive patient data is encrypted and access-controlled.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the software HIPAA compliant?

Yes, all our healthcare software solutions are designed with strict HIPAA and GDPR compliance features including encryption and access controls.

Can you customize for our hospital?

Absolutely. We specialize in bespoke software development tailored to the unique workflows and legacy systems of your institution.
