Frequently Asked Questions

What is a thesis protocol/synopsis?

A thesis protocol or synopsis is a structured plan of your proposed research. It includes the introduction, operational definitions, hypothesis, objectives, review of literature, methodology, and statistical analysis plan. It must be submitted to the ethics committee for approval before starting the study.

Can you help if I have already collected my data?

Yes, we can assist with the statistical analysis of your collected data. Our statisticians will clean the data, perform the appropriate tests, and generate tables and graphs with interpretation.

Is your service plagiarism-free?

Absolutely. We write every document from scratch. We also provide a plagiarism report (e.g., Turnitin) with our deliverables to ensure originality.
