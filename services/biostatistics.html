Biostatistics & Analysis

Turning complex data into robust, statistically significant evidence.

Rigorous Statistical Support

Robust statistical analysis is the backbone of any clinical study. Our team of pharmacometricians and statisticians helps you design studies that are powered correctly and interpret data with precision. We use industry-standard tools (SAS, R, SPSS) to deliver compliant results.

From sample size calculation to final TFLs (Tables, Figures, Listings), we ensure your data stands up to regulatory scrutiny.

Our Analytical Services

  • Statistical Analysis Plans (SAP): Comprehensive plans detailing methodologies before unblinding.
  • Meta-Analysis: Advanced pooling of data from multiple studies for systematic reviews.
  • Sample Size Estimation: Ensuring your study has the power to detect significant effects.
  • SAS Programming: Generating CDISC-compliant datasets (SDTM/ADaM) and TFLs.
  • Thesis & Dissertation Support: Helping PhD candidates with data interpretation and visualization.

Visualizing Your Data

We don't just crunch numbers; we tell a story with data. Our team creates publication-ready graphs (Forest plots, Kaplan-Meier curves, Waterfall plots) that make complex results instantly understandable for reviewers and readers.
