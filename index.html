Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our medical writing services.

Do you offer end-to-end support for Medical Thesis writing?

Yes, we support MD, MS, and DNB students throughout their thesis journey—from protocol writing and ethics committee submission to biostatistical analysis and final thesis drafting tailored to your university guidelines.

Do you provide statistical analysis support?

Yes, our biostatisticians specialize in medical thesis data analysis. We help with study design, sample size calculation, p-value interpretation, and creating publication-ready graphs using SAS/SPSS.

How do you ensure data confidentiality?

We adhere to strict confidentiality agreements (NDAs) with all our clients. Our data management systems are secure and compliant with global data privacy regulations.

Can you help with publishing in specific high-impact journals?

Absolutely. We assist with journal selection based on your study's scope and impact. We then format and edit your manuscript to meet the specific guidelines of that journal to maximize acceptance chances.
