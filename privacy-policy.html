Last updated: March 16, 2026

Home/Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to MedicoWriter. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you visit our website (www.medicowriter.com) and use our professional medical writing services.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect and process the following data about you:

  • Personal Identification Information: Name, email address, phone number provided through our contact forms.
  • Professional Information: Details regarding your research, clinical studies, medical documents, or thesis requirements.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, operating system, and website usage statistics via cookies and analytics tools.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your data for the following purposes:

  • To provide our medical writing and biostatistics services.
  • To directly communicate with you regarding your inquiries, quotes, and project progress.
  • To improve our website's performance and user experience.
  • To comply with legal and regulatory obligations.

4. Data Security and Confidentiality

Confidentiality is a cornerstone of our service. We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal and research data against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. Strict Non-Disclosure Agreements (NDAs) are signed with all clients before project commencement to ensure complete data protection.

5. Third-Party Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your data only with trusted partners assisting us in operating our website or conducting our business, provided those parties agree to keep this information confidential.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to the personal data we hold about you, ask for corrections, or request deletion of your information. To exercise these rights, please contact us at the email provided below.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
