Medical Thesis Rescue

From stalled draft to distinction-level submission in 3 weeks.

Medical Thesis

The Challenge: Statistical Gridlock

A final-year MD student had collected excellent clinical data on post-operative outcomes in laparoscopic surgery but was stuck at the analysis stage.

With only 3 weeks until the strict university submission deadline, the student was struggling with:

  • Selecting the correct statistical tests (Parametric vs. Non-parametric).
  • Interpreting complex P-values using SPSS.
  • Formatting the 150-page document according to the specific university guidelines (MUHS).

Our Solution

MedicoWriter deployed a rapid response team comprising a Biostatistician and a Senior Medical Writer:

  • Data Cleaning & Analysis: Within 48 hours, we cleaned the raw Excel data and ran comprehensive tests (Chi-square, T-tests, and Regression analysis) using SPSS.
  • Results Transformation: We converted the raw outputs into publication-quality tables and graphs.
  • University-Specific Formatting: We reformatted the entire document (citations, margins, TOC) to strictly match the university's thesis protocol.

The Result

3 Days for Analysis
100% Formatting Compliance

The thesis was submitted 5 days before the deadline. The external examiner specifically praised the clarity of the statistical analysis and the discussion section.

"I was panicking about my P-values. Your statistician not only did the analysis but explained it to me so I could defend it during my viva. Lifesavers!"
- Dr. A.K., MD Candidate (General Surgery)
